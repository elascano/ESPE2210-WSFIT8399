#20 Planet Tatooine

Tatooine is a desert planet located in the Outer Rim region of the Star Wars galaxy. It is known for its twin suns, harsh terrain, and its role in the Star Wars franchise as the home planet of Anakin and Luke Skywalker.

Planet Details

  • Location: Outer Rim
  • Climate: Arid desert
  • Terrain: Sand dunes, rocky outcrops, and canyons
  • Inhabitants: Jawas, Tusken Raiders, and other desert-dwelling species
  • Notable features: Mos Eisley spaceport, the Dune Sea, and the Jundland Wastes

Famous Events

  1. The Podrace at Mos Espa, in which Anakin Skywalker wins his freedom from slavery
  2. The Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon, in which Luke Skywalker defeats Jabba the Hutt
