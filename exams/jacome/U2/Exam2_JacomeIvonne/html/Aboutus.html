About US

Mission

In compliance with the content of the Creation of the
Municipal Public Company of Parks, Cemeteries,
Green Areas, Recreation Areas and Cultural Spaces
of Portoviejo, whose purpose is to manage,
develop and administer the parks, cemeteries, green
areas,recreation and cultural spaces of the Quito canton.

Vision

Consolidate Portoparques EP, as a company recognized nationally
for its efficiency and quality of results in the administration of
parks, cemeteries, green areas, recreation areas and cultural
spaces in Portoviejo, with autonomy and economic and financial
sustainability and a team committed to its city and its people,
which promotes respect for the city and its natural environment
and strengthens the identity and sense of belonging of citizens.
