INFORMATIVE ROAD SIGNS

They are blue signals, they have the function, as their name indicates, to inform and at the same time
alert the pedestrian and driver. The most common that can be found can be directional signs,
exclusive parking, bus stop, hospital, gas station, among others.

PREVENTIVE TRAFFIC SIGNS

PREVENTIVE TRAFFIC SIGNS Preventive traffic signs have the function of warning the pedestrian or driver of dangerous situations or not.
Its shape is a yellow rhombus with black images. Among the most common of this type are winding curve, curve,
cliff, limited width, limited height
